Module Poomsae.Trigram

Trigrams are symbols constituted by 3 horizontal lines which may each be broken down into two parts.

Some examples:

--  --
------
------
--  --
------
--  --

Because there are 3 lines with 2 possible choices per line (plain, or broken down into two parts), it follows that there are 8 different trigrams in total.
